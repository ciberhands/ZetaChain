# Delegating to a Validator

To delegate your ZETA tokens to a validator, select the "Stake" tag in Leap:

<Image src="/docs/img/docs/leap-stake.png" width={400} height={597} alt="Leap extension - Staking" />

Choose a validator to delegate to:

<Image src="/docs/img/docs/leap-validator.png" width={400} height={597} alt="Leap extension - Stake tZETA" />

Enter the amount of ZETA you would like to delegate and click "Review":

<Image src="/docs/img/docs/leap-review.png" width={400} height={597} alt="Leap extension - Stake Review" />

Review the transaction details and click "Confirm" to sign and broadcast the
transaction:

<Image src="/docs/img/docs/leap-confirm.png" width={400} height={597} alt="Leap extension - Confirm Stake" />
